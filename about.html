Praised by Simon Groom, Director of Modern and Contemporary Art at the National Galleries of Scotland, as an artist of ‘singular vision’, Robert Powell has exhibited widely both in the UK and abroad. He works across a range of media, including painting, printmaking, sculpture, moving image and installation, and his publications include Shadow Bestiary (2008), Macpherson’s Cave and Praise of Folly (both 2011). Powell’s most recent exhibition was A Universal History of Iniquity, at Engramme in Quebec City, March 2014.
